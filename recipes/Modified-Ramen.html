Description

This recipe is very easy if you already know how to make instant ramen. But instead of eating the ramen as it comes we are going to be adding extra Ingredients to make it more fun.

The fun part of this recipe is that you can add anything you would like. I have in the past added pork carrots and other things to really make any instant ramen feel more like a meal. It really becomes a blank canvas of things that you can add to the ramen.

Ingerdients

  • Your favorite type of Instant Ramen
  • Egg
  • Green Onion
  • Garlic
  • Black Sesame Oil
  • (Opiotnal) Siracha

Steps

  1. Boil your instant ramen noodles to the packages specifications
  2. In a separate Pot poil your egg for 3 min for soft and 5 for hard
  3. Chop Green Onion and Garlic
  4. Once Instant ramen is done add All the extra Ingredients
  5. Finnally top it off with Sesame oil and Siracha If you like
  6. Enjoy!
